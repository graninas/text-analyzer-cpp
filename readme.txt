Text Analyser is the research experimental work. The analyzer was developed as a thesis project. 

- Recognition of authorship based on comparing the characteristics of the text by Hamming neurosystem.
- Euphony map (know-how).

At the moment there is far from an ideal version of outlining the basic principles of authorship recognition and the construction of euphony maps.
The author plans to someday rewrite all the light of new knowledge and skills.

The main development environment is Borland C++ Builder 6.0
